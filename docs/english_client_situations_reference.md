# ENGLISH CLIENT SITUATIONS REFERENCE

## Practical English for difficult client situations, negotiation, escalation, and stakeholder communication

> **Mục đích của tài liệu**
>
> - Giúp bạn xử lý các tình huống khó với khách hàng bằng tiếng Anh đơn giản, lịch sự và chuyên nghiệp
> - Tập trung vào những case thường gây áp lực trong công việc thực tế
> - Ưu tiên mẫu câu an toàn, dễ nói, dễ viết, dễ thay keyword
> - Có giải thích tiếng Việt để bạn hiểu khi nào nên dùng

> **Cách dùng tài liệu**
>
> - Chọn đúng tình huống bạn đang gặp
> - Học theo cụm, không cần học thuộc toàn bộ
> - Nếu tình huống nhạy cảm, ưu tiên câu ngắn và mềm
> - Dùng câu “giữ an toàn” khi bạn chưa chắc hoặc chưa thể cam kết

# 1. TƯ DUY XỬ LÝ TÌNH HUỐNG KHÓ VỚI KHÁCH HÀNG

## 1.1. Mục tiêu

Trong tình huống khó, mục tiêu không phải là nói “hay”.

Mục tiêu là:

- giữ bình tĩnh
- nói rõ điều mình biết
- không hứa quá mức
- giữ thái độ hợp tác
- chốt được bước tiếp theo

## 1.2. Công thức rất an toàn

- **From our side, here is the current status.**
- **We understand your concern.**
- **At the moment, we’re still checking the details.**
- **We’d like to suggest a safer approach.**
- **This may affect the timeline slightly.**
- **We’ll follow up with a clearer update by [time].**

## 1.3. 3 điều rất quan trọng

1. Đừng đoán nếu chưa chắc
2. Đừng nói quá trực tiếp khi cần phản biện
3. Luôn có next action rõ ràng

# 2. SMALL DISAGREEMENT VÀ PHẢN BIỆN MỀM

## 2.1. Khi bạn không hoàn toàn đồng ý

- **I see your point.**
- **I understand the idea.**
- **From our side, we have one concern.**
- **We may need to review this part more carefully.**

## 2.2. Mẫu phản biện mềm

- **I understand the intention, but we may need to consider the impact on [timeline / logic / scope].**
- **That approach could work, but there may be some risk in [area].**
- **From our perspective, option A may be safer than option B.**
- **We’re a bit concerned that this could affect [flow / stability / maintainability].**

## 2.3. Khi muốn không nói “No” quá thẳng

- **I’m not sure that would be the best approach for now.**
- **That may be difficult to implement within the current timeline.**
- **We may need a different approach here.**
- **I think we should review another option as well.**

## 2.4. Ví dụ thực tế

- **I understand your suggestion, but we’re concerned that this may create extra complexity in the current flow.**
- **That is possible, but it may affect the delivery timeline.**
- **We can consider that option, but we’d recommend reviewing the impact first.**

# 3. TỪ CHỐI KHÉO / KHÔNG THỂ NHẬN NGAY

## 3.1. Khi chưa thể đồng ý ngay

- **We can’t confirm that right away.**
- **We need to review this internally first.**
- **We’d like to evaluate the impact before confirming.**

## 3.2. Khi không thể làm trong scope hiện tại

- **This seems to be outside the current scope.**
- **At the moment, this is not included in the agreed scope.**
- **We may need to treat this as a separate request.**

## 3.3. Khi cần từ chối mềm

- **I’m afraid we may not be able to support that in this phase.**
- **It may be difficult for us to handle this within the current plan.**
- **We may need to postpone this item for now.**

---

# 4. NEGOTIATE TIMELINE / SCOPE

## 4.1. Khi cần nói timeline có rủi ro

- **This may affect the current timeline.**
- **We may need a bit more time for this item.**
- **The current estimate may need to be adjusted.**

## 4.2. Khi cần đề xuất đổi scope hoặc priority

- **If this item is urgent, we may need to move another item to the next phase.**
- **To stay on schedule, we may need to reduce the scope slightly.**
- **We’d like to suggest prioritizing [item A] first.**

## 4.3. Khi cần thương lượng

- **If we keep the current scope, we’ll need more time.**
- **If the timeline needs to stay the same, we may need to simplify [feature].**
- **We can support this request, but it may require additional effort.**

## 4.4. Mẫu đầy đủ

- **From our side, we see two options. If we keep the current scope, we’ll need more time. If we need to keep the current deadline, we may need to reduce the scope slightly.**

---

# 5. XIN THÊM THỜI GIAN

## 5.1. Mẫu an toàn

- **We need a bit more time to review this properly.**
- **Could we have until [time/day] to confirm this?**
- **We’d like to ask for a little more time to finalize the details.**

## 5.2. Mẫu lịch sự hơn

- **To avoid giving you incomplete information, we’d appreciate a bit more time to verify this internally.**
- **We want to make sure the solution is correct, so we’d like to ask for a short extension on this item.**

## 5.3. Mẫu ngắn cho chat

- **We need a bit more time on this point. We’ll update you by [time].**

---

# 6. DEMO SẢN PHẨM VÀ WALKTHROUGH MÀN HÌNH

## 6.1. Mở đầu demo

- **Let me walk you through the current flow.**
- **I’ll give you a quick demo of the latest version.**
- **Let me show you how this works step by step.**

## 6.2. Dẫn qua từng màn hình

- **First, we start from this screen.**
- **From here, the user can [action].**
- **Next, we move to the [page / step].**
- **At this point, the system will [action].**

## 6.3. Giải thích ngắn gọn

- **This part is designed to [purpose].**
- **This helps prevent [risk / confusion / invalid input].**
- **This is the main change in the latest version.**

## 6.4. Khi khách hàng hỏi giữa demo

- **That’s a good question. Let me finish this flow first, then I’ll come back to that point.**
- **I’ll cover that in the next step.**

## 6.5. Chốt demo

- **That’s the main flow from our side.**
- **Please let us know if you’d like us to walk through any other scenario.**
- **We’d be happy to show the edge cases as well if needed.**

# 7. PRODUCTION INCIDENT / URGENT ESCALATION

## 7.1. Khi cần báo tình huống khẩn

- **We’d like to raise an urgent issue.**
- **We found a production issue affecting [users / transactions / feature].**
- **The team is actively investigating this now.**

## 7.2. Khi chưa biết đầy đủ

- **At the moment, we’re still checking the root cause.**
- **We don’t want to make assumptions too early.**
- **We’ll share a more detailed update as soon as we confirm the facts.**

## 7.3. Khi cần trấn an

- **The team is already working on this with high priority.**
- **We’re doing our best to minimize the impact.**
- **We’ll keep you updated frequently until this is resolved.**

## 7.4. Khi cần escalation

- **We’re escalating this internally to make sure it gets immediate attention.**
- **We’ve involved the relevant team to speed up the investigation.**

## 7.5. Mẫu đầy đủ

- **We’d like to raise an urgent production issue affecting [feature]. The team is actively investigating it now. At the moment, we’re still confirming the root cause, but we’re treating this as a high-priority item and will keep you updated.**

---

# 8. UAT / ACCEPTANCE / SIGN-OFF

## 8.1. Khi chuẩn bị cho UAT

- **The feature is now ready for UAT.**
- **Please let us know when your team is ready to start UAT.**
- **We’ve prepared the latest build for your review.**

## 8.2. Khi xin feedback sau UAT

- **Please share any feedback or issues you notice during UAT.**
- **We’d appreciate your confirmation once the validation is complete.**

## 8.3. Khi xin acceptance / sign-off

- **If everything looks okay from your side, we’d appreciate your sign-off on this item.**
- **Please let us know if we can consider this item accepted.**
- **Once we receive your confirmation, we’ll move this item to completed.**

## 8.4. Khi còn open points

- **There are still a few open points to address before sign-off.**
- **We’d prefer to complete the remaining fixes before requesting final acceptance.**

# 9. STAKEHOLDER MANAGEMENT THEO TỪNG ĐỐI TƯỢNG

## 9.1. Với sếp / manager / decision maker

Ưu tiên:

- ngắn
- rõ impact
- rõ decision needed

### Mẫu câu

- **Here is the current status and the main risk.**
- **We need your decision on [point].**
- **There are two options, and we recommend option A.**

## 9.2. Với PO / BA

Ưu tiên:

- requirement
- expected behavior
- scope
- priority

### Mẫu câu

- **We’d like to confirm the expected behavior for this scenario.**
- **Could you help us prioritize these items?**

## 9.3. Với QA

Ưu tiên:

- reproducible steps
- expected result
- actual result

### Mẫu câu

- **We were able to reproduce the issue in staging.**
- **The expected result is [result], but the actual result is [result].**

## 9.4. Với dev team

Ưu tiên:

- logic
- technical detail
- ownership
- next action

### Mẫu câu

- **From our side, the main concern is in the validation logic.**
- **Can we align on who will handle this part?**

## 9.5. Với client

Ưu tiên:

- rõ
- bình tĩnh
- business-friendly
- tránh technical detail không cần thiết

### Mẫu câu

- **We found an issue affecting this flow.**
- **We’re working on it now and will keep you updated.**
- **We’d like to confirm the expected business behavior before proceeding.**

# 10. KHI KHÁCH HÀNG HỎI KHÓ HOẶC GÂY ÁP LỰC

## 10.1. Khi chưa có đáp án ngay

- **That’s a valid question. Let me verify it first.**
- **I’d like to confirm the details internally before I answer.**

## 10.2. Khi khách hàng hỏi “Why is this delayed?”

- **We needed more time to validate the solution properly.**
- **We found an issue during implementation, and we didn’t want to move forward without checking the impact carefully.**

## 10.3. Khi khách hàng đòi cam kết nhanh

- **I understand the urgency. At the moment, I don’t want to commit to a timeline before we verify the details.**
- **We’ll confirm the timeline as soon as we complete the internal review.**

## 10.4. Khi khách hàng không hài lòng

- **We understand your concern.**
- **Thank you for raising this clearly.**
- **We take this point seriously and are reviewing it carefully.**

---

# 11. KHI TEAM CHƯA CHẮC NHƯNG CẦN PHẢN HỒI

## 11.1. Câu giữ an toàn

- **At the moment, this is our current understanding.**
- **We’re still validating the details internally.**
- **I don’t want to give you inaccurate information.**
- **We’ll follow up with a confirmed answer shortly.**

## 11.2. Mẫu thực tế

- **From our current understanding, this should be handled in [way]. However, we’d like to confirm it internally first.**

---

# 12. FOLLOW-UP SAU TÌNH HUỐNG NHẠY CẢM

## 12.1. Sau escalation

- **Following up on the issue discussed earlier, here is the latest update from our side.**

## 12.2. Sau disagreement

- **Thanks for the discussion earlier. We reviewed the options again, and here is our recommendation.**

## 12.3. Sau khi xin thêm thời gian

- **As discussed, we reviewed the item internally and would like to share the confirmed update below.**

# 13. CÂU NÊN TRÁNH VÀ CÁCH NÓI TỰ NHIÊN HƠN

## 13.1. Quá thẳng

- **You are wrong.**

### Nên nói

- **I think we may have a different understanding here.**

## 13.2. Hứa quá sớm

- **We can definitely finish this tomorrow.**

### Nên nói

- **We’ll need to confirm the timeline internally first.**

## 13.3. Từ chối cứng

- **We cannot do that.**

### Nên nói

- **That may be difficult within the current scope.**

# 14. MINI CHEAT SHEET

## 14.1. 10 câu rất đáng nhớ

1. **We understand your concern.**
2. **From our side, we have one concern.**
3. **That may affect the current timeline.**
4. **We may need a bit more time for this item.**
5. **We’d like to suggest a safer approach.**
6. **Let me walk you through the current flow.**
7. **We’d like to raise an urgent issue.**
8. **The feature is now ready for UAT.**
9. **We need to confirm this internally first.**
10. **We’ll follow up with a clearer update by [time].**

## 14.2. Công thức cực an toàn

- **Acknowledge** -> **We understand your concern.**
- **Status** -> **Here is the current status from our side.**
- **Risk** -> **This may affect [timeline / scope / quality].**
- **Next step** -> **We’ll follow up by [time].**

# KẾT LUẬN

Những tình huống khó không cần câu quá phức tạp.

Bạn chỉ cần:

- bình tĩnh
- lịch sự
- không hứa quá sớm
- nói rõ status
- chốt next step

> **Mẹo cuối cùng:** Trong tình huống nhạy cảm, câu ngắn và chắc luôn tốt hơn câu dài nhưng không an toàn.
